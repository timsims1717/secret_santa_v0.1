Instructions:

Next, in the option tab, figure out the settings you want in this gift exchange.


Finally, write a custom message for the participants in the gift exchange! This will be included after a greeting.


To look at how your gift exchange email will appear, press PREVIEW. Once you are done, press SUBMIT to send out your gift exchange!
